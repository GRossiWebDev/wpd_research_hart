Clutter Competitive Analysis

Food52 is a blog site for recipes, living, and travel, and tries to push the users to buy items they are selling. The website does have a Q&A area. It is called Hotline.

The site seems lacking in colors, as white and grey are the main two colors used through the site, with little bits of gold used. The gold is used on links and buttons that are not main navigation. There is gold also on a blog's rating.

It isn't lacking in pictures except in the Q&A area. The blogs and home page has pictures everywhere while the Q&A area is only the questions.

The Q&A area is able to be split into urgent, unanswered, and topics. The questions can be answered by anyone. The answers are voted on by others on the site and brings the top most 'liked' comment to the top.

Clutter User Persona

Olivia Miller

Female

73 years old

Retired Banker - Stay at Home Grandmother

Went to community college for finance

Olivia has been baking since she was a child. She only recently started looking online for recipes as she is not very good with technology and prefers paper print outs. She just recently got an iPhone 6 and still prefers her computer over her phone.

Olivia likes to print out the recipes she finds online and texts her friend when she has questions. Her friend use to run a bakery and is only good with helping with deserts. Olivia said she finds herself on FaceBook asking others for help when it comes to dinners and snacks.

Olivia struggled to find a website she liked to help explain her favorite website for recipes. She brought up FaceBook when trying to explain and used it the whole time to explain.

Favorite Websites

  • FaceBook (For enterainment and connecting with others)
  • Google (For questions others cannot answer)

Likes

  • Websites being easy to understand
  • Pictures to explain what may be happening
  • Splashes of color

Dislikes

  • No pictures
  • Pages having too much
  • Too little information
  • Not knowing others opinions on a subject
